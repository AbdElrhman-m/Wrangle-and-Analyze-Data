The WeRateDogs Twitter archive contains basic tweet data for all 5000+ of their tweets, but not everything. One column the archive does contain though: each tweet's text, which I used to extract rating, dog name, and dog "stage" (i.e. doggo, floofer, pupper, and puppo) to make this Twitter archive "enhanced." Of the 5000+ tweets, I have filtered for tweets with ratings only (there are 2066 ).

More than 60% of dog tweets about pupper dogs, more than 30% about doggo. The pupper dogs should be the most popular on twitter! however, the average rating of pupper tweets is 1.07 which is the lowest average rate.
